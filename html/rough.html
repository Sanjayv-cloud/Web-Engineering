Sri Krishna College Of Technology

Nestled at the foothills of the Western Ghats, located in a sprawling 52-acre campus inKovaipudur, Coimbatore, Sri Krishna College of Technology (SKCT) is a
vibrant institute of higher education established in 1985 promoted by Sri Krishna Institutions.

An extraordinary freedom of opportunity to explore, to collaborate and to challenge oneself is the
hallmark of the Institute

The college offers 11 undergraduate programmes and 6 postgraduate programmes in
engineering, technology, and Management Studies
