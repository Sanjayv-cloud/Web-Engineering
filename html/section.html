Article about Sri Krishna College of Technology

Student Grievance Cell functions 24/7 in different forms, which are confided by students. Student council meetings, Class committee meetings, and tutor ward meetings are conducted to gather and act upon student grievances, apart from online and offline feedback.

Students are periodically counseled for their emotional health and well-being by a dedicated in-house Psychological Counselor. Placement training sessions are conducted both by in-house faculty and outsourced resources. Alumni also contribute to placements through the conduction of coaching classes, motivational talks, and mock interviews.

Technical and non-technical clubs are instituted to develop the holistic personality of students. Events like Fine Arts day, Engineer’s day, Sports day, Yoga day, Festival celebrations, etc., are conducted as club activities. Displaying motivational videos and webinars wherein experts share their challenges to inspire the students for field-integrated learning.

Financial support is provided to students through scholarships and to students who go abroad for presenting papers at conferences. 3841 students, accounting for 70.91% of the total student strength, have been placed in reputed companies since 2014. Canteens and Food courts within the campus serve hygienic and healthy foods.

About us

Nestled at the foothills of the Western Ghats, located in a sprawling 52-acre campus in Kovaipudur, Coimbatore, Sri Krishna College of Technology (SKCT) is a vibrant institute of higher education established in 1985 promoted by Sri Krishna Institutions. An extraordinary freedom of opportunity—to explore, to collaborate and to challenge oneself is the hallmark of the Institute. Being an autonomous institute, affiliated to Anna University, Chennai, and approved by AICTE, New Delhi, SKCT lays strong emphasis on collaborative research and stands apart from other institutes by its participatory work culture, student care programmes and high industry interaction. In a span of 38 years, it has emerged as one of the premier engineering colleges for learning, discovery and innovation due to the dynamic leadership of the Chairperson and Managing Trustee Smt S Malarvizhi. Being an acclaimed educationalist, she continues to contribute profusely for the glory and happiness of advancing generations. The college is accredited with A Grade by NAAC and eligible undergraduate programs are accredited by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA), New Delhi. The college offers 11 undergraduate programmes and 6 postgraduate programmes in engineering, technology, and Management Studies.
